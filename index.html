Personal Google Ads Account Automation

This is a private internal project developed for managing and optimizing my own Google Ads accounts through a personal Manager Account (MCC).

Project Purpose

  • Access Google Ads reporting data
  • Automate bid and budget adjustments
  • Monitor campaign performance
  • Improve advertising efficiency

Scope of API Usage

  • All accounts accessed are fully owned and operated by me
  • No third-party access
  • No external client management
  • No SaaS platform or public software offering
  • No resale or redistribution of Google Ads data

Compliance

This tool complies with Google Ads API Terms of Service and is used strictly for internal advertising management purposes. This project is not a commercial product and is not offered to the public.
